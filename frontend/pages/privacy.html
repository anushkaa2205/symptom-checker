Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Medora, we take your privacy and the security of your health data extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide accurate symptom analysis and maintain your health history:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication details.
  • Health Data: Symptoms, health history, and assessment results provided during chat sessions.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our platform to improve our AI models.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used specifically for:

  • Providing personalized AI-powered health insights.
  • Generating downloadable health reports for your doctors.
  • Improving the accuracy of our clinical triaging algorithms.
  • Maintaining your assessment history for long-term tracking.

3. Data Security & Encryption

We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your sensitive information:

  • All health data is encrypted at rest and during transit using industry-standard protocols.
  • We use secure authentication methods to ensure only you can access your history.
  • Regular security audits are conducted to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal health data to third parties. Information is only shared:

  • When you explicitly choose to export and share a report with a healthcare provider.
  • With essential service providers who help us maintain the platform (e.g., secure cloud hosting).
  • If required by law to comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data on Medora:

  • You can access and download your entire assessment history at any time.
  • You can request the deletion of your account and all associated health data.
  • You can update your personal information through your profile settings.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and remember your theme preferences. We do not use invasive tracking for advertising purposes.
